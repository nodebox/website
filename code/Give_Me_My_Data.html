Give Me My Data reclaims and reuses your Facebook data.

Give Me My Data is a Facebook application created by Owen Mundy that helps users export their data out of Facebook. Reasons could include making artwork, archiving and deleting your account, or circumventing the interface Facebook provides. Data can be exported in CSV, XML, and to NodeBox, among others.

http://givememydata.com/
