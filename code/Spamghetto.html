Spamghetto

Have fun with comically misspelled offers of love and enthusiastic advice on how to get rich, fast!

Spamghetto is a wallpaper based on the spam in your mailbox, made by the Italian design studio ToDo. It's printed of FSC-certified paper (environmentally appropriate). It's won silver at the European Design Award 2010.

You can order it online at:
http://www.spamghetto.com/

 

 

Note how the text in the wallpaper follows the curves of the composition. If you are trying to achieve something similar, here is a post on how to fit text to a path.
